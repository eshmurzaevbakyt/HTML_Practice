Lion

The lion is a big cat known for its majestic appearance and is often referred to as the "king of the jungle."

Tiger

Tigers are the largest species of big cats and are known for their distinctive orange coat with black stripes.

Wolf

Wolves are highly social and intelligent predators known for their hunting prowess and pack behavior.

Yuujirou

Superhuman, martial artist, Kayo of all Kayos, highly paid freelance mercenary and assassin for various governments and organizations, father of Bucky Hanma and Jack Hanma.
